# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

career-ops is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful
environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

We expect all contributors and participants to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Enforcement

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its Code of Conduct. All instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported
by opening a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/poferraz/career-ops/issues) or
contacting the maintainer directly via the details in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality of the reporter.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub Issues, PRs,
Discussions) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
